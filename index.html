Overview

Training and Placement

Aligning to our belief and values in enabling an education that empower students technologically, logically and analytically to the dynamic and ever demanding business world, DSU has taken training and placement opportunities to the next level that is more than just an employment.

Leveraging the DSI legacy of five decades, Dayananda Sagar University has a well-established and centralized placement and training division - Centre of Innovation and Leadership (CIL).
Today, Dayananda Sagar Institutions and Dayananda Sagar University have become one of the preferred academic institutions for a large number of organizations to source the best talents under different verticals and horizontals of the business world.

The guidance to every student of the university to sort their career paths starts right from the first semester and walks through them till they reach their career goals. The training programs are custom made to every batch of engineering, commerce, and management, applied science, health science and arts courses. All the training program starts only after the thorough analyzing of student’s capabilities, personality, IQ & EQ of the students.

The placement cell also offers internship programs for undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Of late, Dayananda Sagar University has started with in-house summer internship program for engineering students in areas of IOT & Robotics, 3D printing, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and much more.
