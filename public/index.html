Training and Placements

Aligning to our belief and values in enabling an education that empower students technologically, logically and analytically to the dynamic and ever demanding business world, DSU has taken training and placement opportunities to the next level that is more than just an employment.

Leveraging the DSI legacy of five decades, Dayananda Sagar University has a well-established and centralized placement and training division - Centre of Innovation and Leadership (CIL).

Today, Dayananda Sagar Institutions and Dayananda Sagar University have become one of the preferred academic institutions for a large number of organizations to source the best talents under different verticals and horizontals of the business world.

The guidance to every student of the university to sort their career paths starts right from the first semester and walks through them till they reach their career goals.

The training programs are custom made to every batch of engineering, commerce, and management, applied science, health science and arts courses. All the training program starts only after the thorough analyzing of studentâ€™s capabilities, personality, IQ & EQ of the students.

The placement cell also offers internship programs for undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Programs and Facilities

In today' s vibrant job market, organizations look for core technical skills, excellent functional skills, learning and managerial skills and exceptional outlook in the young talents. Our training programs, includes pre-placement and placement training modules which helps students to know where they stand and where they need improvement.

Our training modules for all the students of undergraduate and post graduate courses aligning to the business needs and employer expectations, we have designed our training modules that teach basic life skills, Communication skills, problem solving skills, leadership & decision-making skills and also pre-placement training such as resume writing, group discussion and mock interviews.

The university is very well equipped to facilitate pre-placement training sessions and placement process in each and every step.

Centre for Innovation & Leadership - CIL

CIL - is established in the year 2006 under the banner of DSI to bridge the gap between pure academics and the corporate realities. The Centre aims to impart innovative soft skill training programs to enable individuals to become champion leaders in their endeavour, and to sustain a high level of performance at the individual and the organizational level.

CIL recognizes the challenge of unleashing the potential of individuals to transform them to achieve excellence. To face the challenge, CIL is armed with eminent Academicians, Coaches, Mentors and Thinkers with hands on experience in the corporate world to design high quality training modules.

The primary aim of all the training modules would be to allow individuals to take control of their lives by re-discovering their potential.
